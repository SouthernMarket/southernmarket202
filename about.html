Our Story

"Every piece tells a story. We're just here to help it find its next chapter."

A Shop Built on Passion

Southern Market 202 was born from a deep love of history, craftsmanship, and the stories that objects carry through time. Tucked in the heart of Williamston, South Carolina, our shop is a carefully curated collection of antiques, vintage finds, and one-of-a-kind pieces that deserve a second life.

We believe that the best things aren't always new. There's a warmth and character in a well-worn piece of furniture, a patina on a brass lamp, or the faded label on a vintage tin that no modern replica can replicate. These objects connect us to the people and places that came before us.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning to discover the world of antiques, we're here to help you find something you'll treasure. Every item in our shop has been hand-selected for its quality, character, and story.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Authenticity

Every item we carry is genuine. We carefully research and verify provenance, so you can trust that what you're bringing home is the real thing — no reproductions, no shortcuts.

02

Southern Warmth

We're a small, family-run shop rooted in the traditions of Southern hospitality. When you walk through our doors — or browse our online collection — we want you to feel at home.

03

Craftsmanship

We have a deep respect for the makers of the past. The pieces we carry represent a standard of quality and care that is increasingly rare — and worth preserving for generations to come.
