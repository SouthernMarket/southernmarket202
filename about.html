About Us

"Making a house a home — the comfortable Southern way."

Southern Market 202 was born in Williamston, SC — a small town with deep roots and a love for things that last. What started as years of collecting beautiful décor and one-of-a-kind pieces grew into something worth sharing.

Every item we carry has been chosen with care — antiques, collectibles and vintage goods that bring warmth, character and history into your home. We believe the right piece doesn't just fill a space, it tells a story.

We're proud to be rooted in Upstate South Carolina, where Southern charm isn't just a saying — it's a way of life.
